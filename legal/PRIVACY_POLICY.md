# Privacy Policy — VulnForge

**Last Updated:** June 29, 2026

## 1. Introduction

VulnForge ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our security audit platform.

We comply with:
- **Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (FADP / nLPD)**
- **EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)** where applicable

## 2. Data We Collect

### 2.1 Account Data
- Email address
- Company name (optional)
- Billing information (processed by Stripe — we do not store credit card numbers)

### 2.2 Scan Data
- Target URLs and specifications
- Scan results (vulnerabilities, findings, reports)
- Scan artifacts (logs, screenshots, request/response data)

### 2.3 Technical Data
- IP address
- Browser type and version
- Timestamps of platform usage

## 3. How We Use Your Data

| Purpose | Data Used | Legal Basis |
|---------|-----------|-------------|
| Provide audit service | Scan data, target specs | Contract performance |
| Account management | Email, company | Contract performance |
| Billing | Email, Stripe customer ID | Contract performance |
| Platform improvement | Anonymized, aggregated scan data | Legitimate interest |
| Legal compliance | All data as required | Legal obligation |

## 4. Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Account data | Until account deletion |
| Scan findings & artifacts | 30 days after scan completion |
| Billing records | 10 years (Swiss legal requirement) |
| Server logs | 90 days |

Clients may request **immediate deletion** of scan data at any time by contacting privacy@vulnforge.io.

## 5. Data Storage & Security

- All data is stored on servers in **Switzerland**
- Data is encrypted **at rest** (AES-256) and **in transit** (TLS 1.3)
- Access to scan data is strictly limited to necessary personnel
- Scan agents run in isolated containers with no persistent access to client targets

## 6. Data Sharing

We do **NOT**:
- Sell your data
- Share your findings with third parties
- Use your data for advertising

We **MAY** share data:
- With Stripe (payment processing) — see Stripe's privacy policy
- If required by Swiss law or court order
- With your explicit consent

## 7. Your Rights

Under FADP/GDPR, you have the right to:
- **Access** — Request a copy of your data
- **Rectification** — Correct inaccurate data
- **Deletion** — Request deletion of your data
- **Portability** — Receive your data in a machine-readable format
- **Objection** — Object to processing based on legitimate interest

To exercise these rights, contact: privacy@vulnforge.io

## 8. Cookies

We use:
- **Essential cookies** — Authentication (JWT in httpOnly cookie)
- **No tracking cookies** — We do not use third-party analytics or advertising cookies

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We will notify users of material changes via email or platform notification. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
