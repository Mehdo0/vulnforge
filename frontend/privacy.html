Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

1. Introduction

VulnForge ("we", "our", or "us") is a cybersecurity audit platform operated by Nous Research. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data transparently and securely. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform.

By accessing VulnForge, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you register, we collect your name, email address, and authentication credentials. We use bcrypt hashing for all passwords — we never store plaintext credentials.

2.2 Scan Targets and Results

When you initiate security audits, we process and store the IP addresses, domains, or URL targets you provide, along with vulnerability scan results, remediation steps, and generated compliance reports.

2.3 Technical Data

We automatically collect standard server logs including IP address, browser type, timestamps, and API request metadata. This data is retained for security monitoring and platform analytics.

2.4 Payment Information

We use Stripe as our payment processor. VulnForge does not store your full credit card details. Stripe handles all payment data in accordance with PCI-DSS standards.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Delivering and improving vulnerability scanning and reporting services
  • Authenticating your access to the platform
  • Processing payments and managing subscriptions
  • Sending service-related notifications (scan completions, billing reminders)
  • Complying with legal obligations and enforcing our Terms of Service
  • Security monitoring, fraud prevention, and abuse detection

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA) and Switzerland, our legal basis for processing your personal data includes:

  • Contractual necessity — processing required to deliver our services to you
  • Legitimate interests — improving platform security and preventing abuse
  • Legal obligation — compliance with applicable laws and regulations
  • Consent — where you have explicitly agreed to specific processing activities

5. Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (FADP / nFADP) Compliance

VulnForge complies with the revised Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (nFADP / revDSG), effective September 1, 2023. We:

  • Process personal data in accordance with the principles of lawfulness, purpose limitation, and proportionality
  • Maintain a record of processing activities (Art. 12 nFADP)
  • Implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures (Art. 8 nFADP)
  • Notify affected individuals and the Federal Data Protection and Information Commissioner (FDPIC) in the event of a data breach (Art. 24 nFADP)
  • Conduct data protection impact assessments for high-risk processing activities

6. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary:

  • Account data — retained until account deletion or 24 months of inactivity
  • Scan results & reports — retained for 90 days after scan completion (or longer for active subscriptions)
  • Server logs — retained for 30 days, then automatically purged
  • Payment records — retained for 10 years per Swiss commercial code (Art. 958f CO)
  • Deleted account data — permanently erased within 30 days of deletion request, except where legal retention obligations apply

7. Data Storage and Transfers

Your data is stored on secure servers located in Switzerland and the European Union. We do not transfer personal data outside these jurisdictions without adequate safeguards (such as Standard Contractual Clauses or adequacy decisions).

8. Your Rights

Under GDPR and Swiss data protection law, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure — request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction — limit processing under certain conditions
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdraw consent — revoke previously given consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@vulnforge.io. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Cookies

VulnForge uses essential cookies for authentication and session management. No third-party tracking or advertising cookies are deployed. For details, see our Cookie Policy.

10. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security practices:

  • All data in transit is encrypted via TLS 1.3
  • Passwords are hashed with bcrypt (cost factor 12)
  • API access is rate-limited and requires authentication tokens
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Principle of least privilege for internal data access

11. Children's Privacy

VulnForge is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If we become aware that a minor has provided us with personal data, we will delete it immediately.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via email or a prominent notice on the platform. Continued use of VulnForge after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
