Our Mission

Modern applications ship at breakneck speed, but security testing hasn't kept pace. Traditional pentests take weeks and produce PDF reports nobody reads. VulnForge changes that.

We combine large language models with battle-tested scanning engines to deliver real-time vulnerability assessments, prioritized by exploitability, with plain-English remediation steps that developers can act on immediately.

Our mission is simple: make continuous security testing as routine as CI/CD. Every scan produces a living report — not a stale document — with automatic retesting and regression detection. We believe security should be a feedback loop, not a checkpoint.
