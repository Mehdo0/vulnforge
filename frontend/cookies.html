Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by websites you visit. They are widely used to make websites function efficiently and provide information to the site owners. Cookies may be "session cookies" (deleted when you close your browser) or "persistent cookies" (remain until they expire or you delete them).

This policy explains how VulnForge uses cookies and similar technologies, and what choices you have regarding them.

2. How We Use Cookies

VulnForge uses strictly necessary cookies only. We do not deploy:

  • Third-party tracking or advertising cookies
  • Analytics cookies (Google Analytics, etc.)
  • Social media tracking pixels
  • Fingerprinting or behavioral profiling scripts

All cookies served by VulnForge are first-party cookies essential for the secure operation of the Platform.

4. Third-Party Services

The only third-party service integrated into our platform is Stripe for payment processing. Stripe may set its own essential cookies during the checkout process to prevent fraud and process payments securely. These cookies are governed by Stripe's Cookie Policy.

5. Managing Cookies

5.1 Browser Settings

Most browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:

  • View and delete existing cookies
  • Block all cookies or third-party cookies
  • Clear cookies when you close the browser
  • Set site-specific cookie preferences

Instructions for popular browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

5.2 Impact of Disabling Cookies

Since VulnForge relies on session cookies for authentication, disabling cookies will prevent you from logging into the Platform. Functional cookies (vulnforge_prefs, vulnforge_cookie_consent) are optional and blocking them will not affect core functionality.

6. Legal Basis

Under the EU ePrivacy Directive (Cookie Law) and GDPR, our essential cookies do not require consent because they are strictly necessary for the service you have requested. Functional cookies are set only upon your explicit interaction (e.g., changing dashboard preferences).

Under Swiss law, our cookie practices are compliant with the revised Federal Act on Data Protection (nFADP) and the Telecommunications Act (TCA).

7. Changes to This Policy

We will notify you of any material changes to this Cookie Policy via the Platform. If we ever introduce non-essential cookies, we will request your explicit consent before setting them.

8. Contact

For questions about our cookie practices, contact us at privacy@vulnforge.io or through our Contact page.
